About LTL&G

LTL&G — Lawn, Trees, Leaves & Gardening — is more than a name. It's a promise.

Born from a vision of natural excellence, LTL&G was founded by Joshua Taylor Herrell to bring bold beauty, precision care, and lasting value to every yard and landscape we touch.

Backed by Johnny Robert Herrell III, our dedicated financial partner, we've invested in top-tier tools, cutting-edge practices, and a customer-first approach that puts your satisfaction at the center of everything we do.

We proudly serve homes and businesses across Kingfisher, W. Canadian, Blaine, and SW Logan counties — delivering services that transform ordinary outdoor spaces into curated works of living art.

From scheduled lawn maintenance to breathtaking garden makeovers, LTL&G is Oklahoma’s new gold standard in green excellence. When your property deserves the best — Hire Us to stand out from the rest...
